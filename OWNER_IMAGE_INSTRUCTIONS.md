# Owner Image Setup Instructions

## How to Add Your Owner Image

1. **Save your image file:**
   - Save your owner photo as `owner.jpg`
   - Place it in the `static/images/` folder
   - Full path: `Desi_Fresh_Milk/static/images/owner.jpg`

2. **Image Requirements:**
   - **Recommended size:** 600px width × 800px height (or similar portrait ratio)
   - **Format:** JPG, PNG, or WebP
   - **File size:** Under 500KB for best performance
   - **Aspect ratio:** Portrait (vertical) works best

3. **Image Cropping Tips:**
   - The image will be automatically cropped to fit the frame
   - Focus should be on the person (centered)
   - The frame shows approximately 300px × 400px
   - Make sure the face is clearly visible and well-lit

4. **If you don't have the image yet:**
   - The website will automatically use a placeholder image
   - You can add your image anytime and it will appear automatically

## Image Optimization Tools

You can use these free online tools to crop/resize your image:
- **Photopea** (https://www.photopea.com) - Free online Photoshop alternative
- **Canva** (https://www.canva.com) - Easy image editing
- **TinyPNG** (https://tinypng.com) - Compress images without losing quality

## Current Image Path

The website looks for the image at:
```
static/images/owner.jpg
```

Once you add the image file, refresh your browser to see it!
